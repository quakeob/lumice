Born from Ice & Light

Where frozen architecture meets the aurora

Deep in Finnish Lapland, LUMICE rises each winter as a cathedral of ice and light. Sculptors, architects and lighting artists shape thousands of tonnes of crystal-clear ice into immersive spaces that glow from within.

Walk through luminous corridors, explore frozen halls, and witness the Northern Lights from a place that exists for only one season.
